<p><p>Retractable needle safety syringes are medical devices that are designed to prevent accidental needlestick injuries. There are two types of retractable needle safety syringes: automatic and manual. Automatic retractable needle safety syringes have a built-in mechanism that automatically retracts the needle into the syringe barrel after the injection is completed. On the other hand, manual retractable needle safety syringes require the healthcare professional to manually retract the needle into the syringe barrel after the injection. Both types aim to enhance healthcare worker safety and reduce the risk of needlestick injuries.</p></p>
