<p><p>Competitive Intelligence Software is available in two main market types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and runs on the user's local servers. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, accessible via the internet. It offers cost-effectiveness, flexibility, and scalability, as well as automatic updates.
