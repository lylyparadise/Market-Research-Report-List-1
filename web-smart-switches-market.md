<p><p>Web-Smart Switches are network switches that offer advanced features and functionalities compared to unmanaged switches, but do not have all the capabilities of fully managed switches. They provide basic management capabilities like VLAN support, Quality of Service (QoS) settings, and port mirroring, making them ideal for small to medium-sized businesses that need more control over their network without the complexity of fully managed switches.</p><p>The Web-Smart Switches Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing adoption of Web-Smart switches in various industries such as healthcare, education, and retail, where there is a growing demand for network infrastructure with advanced management capabilities. Additionally, the rising trend of cloud computing and the Internet of Things (IoT) is driving the need for more flexible and scalable network solutions, further fueling the demand for Web-Smart switches.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Web-Smart Switches Market include the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) algorithms for network optimization, the development of energy-efficient switches to reduce carbon footprint, and the emergence of Software-Defined Networking (SDN) solutions for centralized network management and control. These trends are expected to drive innovation and growth in the Web-Smart Switches Market in the coming years.</p></p>
