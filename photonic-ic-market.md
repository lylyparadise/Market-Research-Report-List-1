<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Photonic IC market is comprised of three major types of integration: monolithic integration, hybrid integration, and module integration. Monolithic integration involves fabricating all the components, such as lasers and photodetectors, on a single silicon wafer. Hybrid integration combines different elements, such as lasers from one material and waveguides from another, into a single device. Module integration involves packaging individual components, like lasers and modulators, into a single module. These integration types offer diverse approaches for designing and manufacturing photonic integrated circuits to meet different application requirements.</p></p>
