<p><p>IC Packing Tray market types can be categorized into MPPE, PES, PS, ABS, and others. MPPE (Modified Polyphenylene Ether) trays are known for their high heat resistance and durability, making them suitable for handling semiconductor components. PES (Polyethersulfone) trays are popular due to their excellent chemical resistance and dimensional stability. PS (Polystyrene) trays are widely used for their low cost and good electrical insulation properties. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) trays are known for their strength and impact resistance. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various tray materials depending on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
