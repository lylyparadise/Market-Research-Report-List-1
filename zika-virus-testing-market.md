<p><p>Zika Virus Testing Market encompasses two main types of tests, namely Molecular Tests and Serologic Tests. Molecular tests detect the presence of Zika virus genetic material in a person's blood, urine, or saliva sample, providing quick and accurate results. Serologic tests, on the other hand, detect antibodies produced by the body in response to a Zika virus infection. These tests are used to identify past exposure to the virus and are particularly useful for pregnant women in assessing their risk of transmitting the virus to their unborn child.</p></p>
