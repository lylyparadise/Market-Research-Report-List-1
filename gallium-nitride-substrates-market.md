<p><p>The Gallium Nitride (GaN) Substrates market includes various types such as GaN on Sapphire, GaN on Si, GaN on GaN, and Other. GaN on Sapphire refers to the integration of GaN layers on a sapphire substrate, offering advantages like good thermal properties and high crystal quality. GaN on Si involves the growth of GaN layers on silicon substrates, enabling compatibility with existing silicon technology. GaN on GaN signifies the use of GaN substrates to grow GaN epitaxial layers, resulting in excellent performance. Lastly, the "Other" market category includes alternative substrate options for GaN-based devices.</p></p>
