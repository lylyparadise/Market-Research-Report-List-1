<p><p>Vapor traps are used to capture and remove harmful vapors or gases from various sources. Activated carbon vapor traps utilize activated carbon to adsorb the vapors and gases, effectively trapping them. They are commonly used in industries such as chemical manufacturing and oil refineries. On the other hand, cold vapor traps rely on cooling technology to condense the vapors, which are then collected and disposed of. These traps are often used in laboratories during experiments involving volatile substances. Both types of vapor traps offer efficient solutions for the safe handling and disposal of vapors and gases.</p></p>
