<p><strong>The Brachytherapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Dose Rate</li><li>Low Dose Rate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brachytherapy equipment market consists of two types: High Dose Rate (HDR) and Low Dose Rate (LDR). HDR brachytherapy involves the temporary placement of a radioactive source for a short period of time. It delivers a high dose of radiation to the tumor target area, ensuring precise treatment. On the other hand, LDR brachytherapy involves the placement of a radioactive source for a longer duration, providing a continuous low dose radiation to the target area. Both types have their unique advantages and are used based on the specific needs and characteristics of the patient's condition.</p></p>
