<p><p>A power puller, also known as a come along or cable puller, is a hand-operated device used for pulling, tensioning, and lifting heavy objects. It is commonly used in construction, automotive, and industrial applications. The power puller consists of a steel cable or chain, a ratchet mechanism, and a handle. By turning the handle, tension is applied to the cable, allowing the user to exert force and move or lift heavy loads.</p><p>The power puller market is projected to experience substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for power pullers from various end-use industries, such as construction, mining, and transportation, is one of the key factors contributing to market growth. The power puller's ability to provide high pulling capacity and ease of use makes it a preferred choice among end-users.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing focus on improving productivity and efficiency in industrial operations is expected to drive the demand for power pullers. These devices assist in reducing manual labor, minimizing the risk of injury, and enhancing overall work efficiency.
