<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) refers to the integration of solar panels into building materials, providing dual functionality. Single Crystal Silicon, Polycrystalline Silicon, and Thin Film are different types of solar panels used in BIPV systems. Single Crystal Silicon panels are made from a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency but higher costs. Polycrystalline Silicon panels are made from multiple crystals, offering good efficiency at a lower cost. Thin Film panels are made by depositing a thin layer of semiconductor material on a substrate, offering lower efficiency but flexibility and lower costs.</p></p>
