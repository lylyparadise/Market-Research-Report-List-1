<p><p>Vertical Metal Machining Center is a type of machine used in the manufacturing sector for metalworking operations. The market for these machines can be categorized into three types - 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis. In a 3-axis machining center, the cutting tool moves along three axes of the machine. A 4-axis machine adds an additional rotary axis, offering more flexibility in machining complex parts. On the other hand, a 5-axis machine has two rotary axes, allowing for even greater precision and versatility in metal machining operations.</p></p>
