<p><p>The fencing mask market consists of three main types: foil, epee, and sabre masks. Foil masks are typically lighter and provide better visibility, making them suitable for fast-paced movements. Epee masks are heavier and offer more head protection, as they are used for thrusting movements. Sabre masks cover the entire head and are designed to withstand powerful hits and full-arm slashes. Each type targets specific fencing disciplines and has unique features to ensure safety and improve performance for fencers of different styles and preferences.</p></p>
