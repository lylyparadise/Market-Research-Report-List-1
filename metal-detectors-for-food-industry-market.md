<p><p>Metal detectors for the food industry are available in different market types. Search heads are standalone units that can be manually manipulated to scan products for metal contaminants. Conveyor-based systems consist of a conveyor belt that moves products through the metal detector. Pipeline metal detectors are used for pumped products, where the product flows through a pipeline and is scanned for metal contaminants. Gravity feed metal detectors are designed to scan products that are dropped or free-fallen, ensuring they are free from metal contaminants.</p></p>
