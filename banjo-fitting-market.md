<p><p>Banjo fitting is a type of threaded fitting used in hydraulic systems to connect hoses, pipes, and other hydraulic components. It consists of a hollow bolt with a hollow spherical connection at one end and a hollow cylindrical connection at the other end. The fitting is designed to allow fluid flow through both the bolt and the spherical connection, providing a 360-degree range of motion for the connected component. Banjo fittings are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, agriculture, and industrial machinery.</p><p>The banjo fitting market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years, and this growth is expected to continue during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Banjo Fitting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Elbow</li><li>T Shape</li><li>Y Shape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Banjo fittings are commonly used in fluid systems to connect hoses and pipes. Linear banjo fittings have a straight design, allowing for a direct flow of fluid. Elbow banjo fittings have a curved shape, enabling fluid to change direction smoothly. T shape banjo fittings have one input and two outputs, facilitating branching of fluid flow. Y shape banjo fittings have one input and three outputs, allowing fluid to be distributed to multiple locations. These different market types of banjo fittings accommodate various fluid system requirements, providing flexibility in design and installation.</p></p>
