<p><p>RF Coaxial Cable Assemblies market types can be segmented into semi-rigid, semi-flexible, and flexible cables. Semi-rigid cables have solid outer conductors, providing excellent shielding performance and stability, making them ideal for applications requiring precision. Semi-flexible cables have a stranded outer conductor, offering moderate flexibility while maintaining good electrical performance. They are suitable for applications requiring some flexibility. Flexible cables have multiple layers of shielding and a flexible outer conductor, providing excellent flexibility but sacrificing some electrical performance. They are commonly used in applications requiring high flexibility and mobility.</p></p>
