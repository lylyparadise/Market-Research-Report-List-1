<p><p>Pharmaceutical processing seals are essential components used in the pharmaceutical industry to ensure tight and secure connections between various processing equipment. The market for these seals can be categorized into different types. O-Ring seals are circular in shape and are used to create a reliable seal between two parts. Gaskets are flat seals commonly used in pipe connections. Lip seals have a lip on one side to prevent leakage. D seals are shaped like the letter 'D' and provide effective sealing. Other types of seals may include custom-designed solutions to meet specific pharmaceutical processing requirements.</p></p>
