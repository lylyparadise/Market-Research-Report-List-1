In the healthcare sector, cold plasma is used for wound healing, cancer treatment, and sterilization of medical devices. <p><p>The cold plasma market consists of two main types: atmospheric cold plasma and low-pressure cold plasma. Atmospheric cold plasma refers to the utilization of non-equilibrium plasma at atmospheric pressure levels. It finds applications in various industries such as electronics, textiles, and food packaging. On the other hand, low-pressure cold plasma operates at reduced pressures and is primarily used in industries like medical devices and semiconductors. Both types offer different benefits and are employed in different sectors for their unique applications.</p></p>
