<p><strong>The Embedded Substrate(ETS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Passive Substrate(EPS)</li><li>Embedded Trace Substrate(ETS)</li><li>Embedded Dies Substrate(EDS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded Substrate (ETS) refers to a type of substrate technology that integrates different components on a single board. There are different market types within ETS, including Embedded Passive Substrate (EPS), Embedded Trace Substrate (ETS), and Embedded Dies Substrate (EDS). EPS involves embedding passive components like resistors and capacitors within the substrate, reducing the overall size and complexity of the circuit. ETS focuses on embedding high-speed signal traces within the substrate, enhancing signal integrity and reducing electromagnetic interference. EDS involves embedding active components or dies within the substrate, enabling compact and efficient electronic systems.</p></p>
