<p><p>The market for Oily Water Separators (OWS) in the marine industry can be classified into four major types. </p><p>1. Gravity plate separator: This type of OWS uses gravity and specially designed plates to separate oil from water. It is a cost-effective option that relies on physical separation.</p><p>2. Electrochemical: These OWS use an electrochemical process to break down oil and separate it from water. They are more efficient and offer better oil removal rates compared to gravity plate separators.</p><p>3. Bioremediation: This type of OWS utilizes biological agents such as bacteria to degrade and remove oil from the water. It is an environmentally friendly option that helps in the natural breakdown of oil.</p><p>4. Centrifugal: Centrifugal OWS employ high-speed rotating disks to separate oil from water based on their density. They are highly efficient and can handle large volumes of oily water.</p><p>Each type has its advantages and serves different purposes, catering to various needs in the marine industry.</p></p>
