Electromagnetic contactors function by using an electromagnet to open or close the contact points, while permanent magnet contactors utilize a permanent magnet to perform the same function. Both types serve the purpose of controlling electrical currents, but differ in their mechanisms of operation. These contactors play a crucial role in various industries for the efficient and safe operation of electrical systems.</p></p>
