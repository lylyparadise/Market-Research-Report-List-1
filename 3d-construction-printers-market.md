<p><p>3D Construction Printers market consists of two main types, namely Extrusion and Powder Bonding. Extrusion technology involves the deposition of molten material layer by layer to create a three-dimensional object. This method is commonly used with materials like concrete or plastic. On the other hand, Powder Bonding technology utilizes a powdered material, such as cement or gypsum, which is solidified layer by layer using a binding agent, such as water or adhesive. Both methods enable the creation of complex structures with high precision and are revolutionizing the construction industry.</p></p>
