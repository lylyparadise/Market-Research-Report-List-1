<p><p>A side channel pump, also known as a regenerative pump, is a type of centrifugal pump that utilizes the regenerative effect to create pressure. It operates by transferring kinetic energy from the impeller to the pumped liquid. Side channel pumps are characterized by their ability to handle liquids with low vapor pressure, high gas content, or liquids possessing a high affinity for dissolved gases. They are widely used in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical, pharmaceutical, and food and beverage.</p><p>The side channel pump market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving the market growth include the increasing demand for side channel pumps in the oil and gas industry, rising adoption of regenerative pumps in the chemical sector, and growing usage of these pumps in wastewater treatment plants. Additionally, the need for efficient and reliable pumping solutions, along with the increasing focus on energy-efficient systems, is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends that are shaping its landscape. One of the key trends is the integration of advanced technologies, such as IoT and automation, into side channel pumps.
