<p><p>The radiation protective screens market comprises two main types: fixed screens and mobile protective screens. Fixed screens are permanently installed in a specific location such as hospitals or laboratories to shield individuals from radiation exposure during procedures. On the other hand, mobile protective screens are portable and can be easily moved to different locations as required. These screens are also designed to prevent radiation exposure and are commonly used in medical imaging departments, dental practices, and industrial settings. Both fixed and mobile protective screens are essential in ensuring safety and minimizing radiation risks.</p></p>
