<p><p>The egg processing market consists of three main types: dried egg products, liquid egg products, and frozen egg products. </p><p>Dried egg products involve removing the moisture from eggs to create powdered forms that can be used in baking, food products, and foodservice applications. </p><p>Liquid egg products are made by breaking eggs, removing shells, and then pasteurizing and packaging the liquid contents. They are commonly used in food manufacturing industries. </p><p>Frozen egg products are created by freezing liquid egg products, maintaining their freshness, and enabling longer storage periods. These products are often used in foodservice establishments and industrial kitchens.</p></p>
