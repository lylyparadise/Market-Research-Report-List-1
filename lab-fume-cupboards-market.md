<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Lab Fume Cupboards are specialized equipment used in laboratories to contain and remove harmful gases, vapors, and fumes produced during experiments. Conventional Fume Cupboards are the most commonly used type, with a constant flow of outside air drawn into the cupboard and then expelled. Auxiliary Air Fume Cupboards provide additional airflow for certain applications that require higher levels of air control. Re-circulatory Fume Cupboards, on the other hand, recycle air through filters, removing contaminants before releasing it back into the laboratory. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and efficiency of laboratory environments.</p></p>
