<p><p>Piezoelectric sounders are electronic components that generate sound through the conversion of electrical energy into mechanical vibrations. These sounders consist of a piezoelectric crystal or ceramic material that deforms in response to an electrical signal, producing sound waves. They are commonly used in various applications such as alarm systems, electronic devices, medical equipment, and automotive electronics.</p><p>The piezoelectric sounders market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The piezoelectric sounders market can be classified into two types: with signal generators and without signal generators. With signal generators, the sounders are equipped with internal signal generating circuits that produce sound signals when activated. These sounders can be easily controlled and adjusted using external devices. On the other hand, without signal generators, the sounders require an external signal source to produce sound. These sounders are simpler in design and may offer limited control options. Both types cater to different applications and user requirements in various industries.</p></p>
