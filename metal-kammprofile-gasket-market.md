Fixed Guide Ring Kammprofile gaskets have a solid metal ring and are commonly used in applications where high bolt loads are required. On the other hand, Floating Guide Ring Kammprofile gaskets have a flexible metal ring that allows for radial movement, making them suitable for flanges with misalignment or thermal expansion.
