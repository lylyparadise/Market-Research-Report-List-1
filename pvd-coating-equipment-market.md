<p><strong>The PVD Coating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation Coating Machine</li><li>Sputtering Film Coater</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVD Coating Equipment Market is comprised of various types, including Evaporation Coating Machine, Sputtering Film Coater, and Others. Evaporation Coating Machine utilizes a heat source to evaporate materials, which then condense on the substrate and form a film. On the other hand, Sputtering Film Coater employs a sputtering process to dislodge atoms from a target material and deposit them onto the substrate's surface. The "Other" category encompasses additional types of equipment used for PVD coating, which may include specialized machines, new technologies, or hybrid systems.</p></p>
