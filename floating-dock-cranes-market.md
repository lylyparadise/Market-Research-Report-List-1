<p><p>Single boom floating dock cranes and double boom floating dock cranes are two different types of cranes used in dockyards and ports for lifting heavy loads from ships to the dock or vice versa. Single boom cranes have one extendable arm that can rotate to reach and lift the cargo, while double boom cranes have two arms that can work simultaneously or independently to handle larger loads. These cranes play a vital role in the efficient and safe movement of goods between ships and land, enhancing the productivity of dock operations.</p></p>
