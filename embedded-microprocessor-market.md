<p><p>The market for embedded microprocessors can be categorized into two types: ARM-based MPUs and X86-based MPUs. ARM-based MPUs are processors that follow the ARM architecture and are commonly used in mobile devices, IoT devices, and low-power applications. They are known for their energy efficiency and small form factor. On the other hand, X86-based MPUs are processors that follow the x86 architecture and are typically found in personal computers, servers, and high-performance applications. They are known for their compatibility with existing software and higher processing power.</p></p>
