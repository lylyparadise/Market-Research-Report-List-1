<p><p>Helical rotor pumps, also known as progressive cavity pumps or Moyno pumps, are a type of positive displacement pump that is commonly used for handling viscous fluids. They consist of a helical rotor that rotates inside a stator, creating a series of cavities that move the fluid from the inlet to the outlet of the pump.</p><p>The market for helical rotor pumps has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a rapid pace during the forecast period. The increasing demand for helical rotor pumps can be attributed to their ability to handle a wide range of fluids, including high-viscosity fluids, solids-laden slurries, and shear-sensitive materials.</p><p>One of the major factors driving the market growth is the expansion of various end-user industries such as oil and gas, wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and chemical processing. Helical rotor pumps are widely used in these industries for applications such as transferring fluids, dosing, metering, and process circulation.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in pump technology, such as the use of advanced materials and improved designs, have enhanced the performance and efficiency of helical rotor pumps. These pumps offer benefits such as low maintenance, high reliability, and energy efficiency, which further contribute to their growing popularity in the market.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards the adoption of smart pumping solutions. Manufacturers are integrating sensors, monitoring systems, and automation technologies into helical rotor pumps to enhance their functionality and performance. Additionally, the development of compact and portable helical rotor pumps is also gaining traction, particularly in industries where space is a constraint.</p><p>Overall, the helical rotor pumps market is expected to experience robust growth in the coming years. The increasing demand from various end-user industries, coupled with advancements in pump technology, will continue to drive market growth. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period.</p></p>
