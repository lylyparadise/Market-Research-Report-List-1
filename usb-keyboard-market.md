<p><p>The USB keyboard market is divided into two main types: mechanical keyboards and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards are known for their durable construction and individual switches under each key, providing a satisfying tactile feedback. They are popular among gamers and typists who value accuracy and responsiveness. On the other hand, membrane keyboards use a pressure pad beneath the keys to register keystrokes. They are cost-effective and quieter, but may lack the same level of durability and key response as mechanical keyboards.</p></p>
