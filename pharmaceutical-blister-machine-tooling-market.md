<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Blister Machine Tooling market can be segmented into different types based on their functions. Forming tools are used to create the cavities in the blister pack where the medication is placed. Seal tools are responsible for sealing the blister pack. Guide tracks or rails ensure proper alignment of the tooling. Perforation & scoring dies are utilized to create easy-open features on the blister pack. Other types of tooling may include cutting tools or embossing dies for branding or identification purposes.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Thermoform</li><li>Cold-form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical blister machine tooling for thermoforming applications involves forming plastic sheets into blister packs that are then sealed with a foil or film. On the other hand, cold-forming involves the use of aluminum or laminate material that is molded into cavities without the need for heat. Both thermoform and cold-form technologies are widely used in the pharmaceutical industry for packaging tablets, capsules, and other solid dosage forms, providing protection, tamper resistance, and convenience for consumers.</p></p>
