<p><p>The military monocular market consists of two main types: Image Intensifier and Thermal Image monoculars. Image Intensifiers enhance existing light sources, including moonlight and starlight, to provide clear images in low-light conditions. These monoculars are often used for night vision and offer a black and white display. On the other hand, Thermal Image monoculars operate by detecting heat signatures and creating an image based on temperature variations. These monoculars are ideal for detecting hidden objects, such as enemies or equipment, in darkness or through various obstacles.</p></p>
