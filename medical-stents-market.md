<p><p>Medical stents are devices used to keep blood vessels, ducts, or other body passageways open. The market for medical stents can be categorized into various types. Peripheral stents are used to treat blockages in arteries outside the heart, while coronary stents are used to treat blockages in the heart. Prostate stents are designed for patients with prostate enlargement, while urethral stents are used to treat urinary obstructions. Biliary stents are used to treat blockages in the bile ducts, while intracranial stents are used in the brain. Vena cava stents are used to treat blockages in the large vein that carries blood from the body to the heart, and pancreatic stents are used to treat blockages in the pancreas. Each type of stent caters to different medical conditions.</p></p>
