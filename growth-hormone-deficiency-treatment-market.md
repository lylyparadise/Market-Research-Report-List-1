<p><p>Growth hormone deficiency (GHD) treatment is a medical therapy aimed at correcting the inadequate production or secretion of growth hormone (GH) in the body. GHD can occur in both children and adults, leading to various physical and developmental abnormalities. </p><p>The treatment options for GHD include hormone replacement therapy with synthetic GH. This involves the subcutaneous injection of recombinant human GH to stimulate growth and development. The therapy is typically administered for an extended period of time until the individual reaches the desired height or until growth plates close. Other treatment modalities may include concomitant administration of other hormones, such as thyroid hormone, to optimize growth outcomes.</p><p>The Growth Hormone Deficiency Treatment Market is experiencing significant growth due to various factors.
