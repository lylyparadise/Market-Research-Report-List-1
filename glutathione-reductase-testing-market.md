<p><p>Glutathione reductase testing is used to measure the activity of glutathione reductase enzyme in the body, which plays a crucial role in protecting cells from oxidative damage. Different methods are employed for this testing. </p><p>The colorimetric method involves the use of a colored compound that reacts with glutathione reductase to produce a color change, which can be measured using a spectrophotometer. </p><p>The spectrophotometric method measures the absorption of light by glutathione reductase, with higher absorption indicating higher enzyme activity. </p><p>ELISA, or enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay, utilizes specific antibodies to detect and quantify glutathione reductase in a sample, offering high sensitivity and precision.</p></p>
