<p><p>Coreless brushed motor refers to a type of electric motor that comprises two main subcategories: cylindrical motors and disc motors. Cylindrical motors are characterized by their cylindrical shape and consist of an outer housing, armature, and permanent magnets. They offer compact size, high power density, and efficient operation. On the other hand, disc motors feature a flat disc-shaped rotor and provide advantages such as low inertia, fast response time, and quiet operation. Both types find applications in various industries, including drones, robotics, medical devices, and automotive components.</p></p>
