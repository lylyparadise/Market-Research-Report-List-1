<p><p>Coolant valve market types mainly consist of 2-way, 3-way, and others. A 2-way coolant valve is designed to control the flow of coolant between two ports, typically for on/off applications. A 3-way coolant valve, on the other hand, has three ports and can direct the flow of coolant between any two ports. It offers more flexibility and control in diverting the coolant. The "others" category includes coolant valve types that may have more than three ports or unique designs to meet specific application requirements.</p></p>
