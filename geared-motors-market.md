<p><strong>The Geared Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Worm Type</li><li>Planetary Type</li><li>Helical Type</li><li>Helical Bevel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geared motors refer to specific types of motors that have an integrated gearbox. There are four common types in the market. Worm type geared motors use a worm gear to achieve high gear reduction ratios, making them suitable for low-speed applications. Planetary type geared motors consist of multiple gears that revolve around a central sun gear, providing high torque output and precision. Helical type geared motors utilize helical gears for smooth, quiet operation and high efficiency. Lastly, helical bevel type geared motors combine the advantages of helical and bevel gears, offering high torque density and flexibility.</p></p>
