<p><p>Brain tumor treatment market includes various types of treatments, such as chemotherapy and targeted therapy. Chemotherapy involves the use of drugs to kill or stop the growth of cancer cells. It is administered orally or through intravenous infusion. Targeted therapy, on the other hand, focuses on specific molecules or genes involved in the growth of tumor cells. It inhibits their function, preventing the tumor from growing further. Both chemotherapy and targeted therapy are important treatment options in the brain tumor market and are used depending on the characteristics of the tumor and the patient's condition.</p></p>
