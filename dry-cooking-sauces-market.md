<p><p>Dry cooking sauces refer to powdered or granulated products that are used to enhance the flavor of food during cooking. Dehydrated sauces are dry mixtures of herbs, spices, and other ingredients that can be rehydrated with water or other liquids. Gravy mixtures are dry blends of flour, seasonings, and other ingredients that can be mixed with liquid to create a smooth gravy. Pre-made gravy granules are dry, pre-packaged mixes that only require the addition of water or stock to create ready-to-use gravy. These products provide convenience and variety to consumers in the preparation of savory dishes.</p></p>
