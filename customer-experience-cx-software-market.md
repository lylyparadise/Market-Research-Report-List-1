<p><p>Customer Experience (CX) Software is available in two market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise CX software is installed locally on a company's hardware and infrastructure, providing full control and customization but requiring maintenance and security updates by the organization. Cloud-Based CX software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates while minimizing hardware costs. Each option has its own advantages and considerations, allowing businesses to choose based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
