<p><p>High Density Interconnect (HDI) PCBs, also known as microvia technology, are advanced printed circuit boards (PCBs) that feature densely packed components and interconnects. They provide improved signal integrity and high-speed capabilities, making them ideal for applications in industries like telecommunications, healthcare, automotive, and aerospace.</p><p>The high-density interconnect (HDI) PCBs market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
