<p><p>The confectionery wrappers market encompasses various types of materials used for packaging sweets and candies. Cast polypropylene (CPP) is a popular choice due to its excellent tensile strength and moisture barrier properties. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) is commonly used for its transparency, durability, and ability to preserve product freshness. Biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP) films offer high clarity, good stiffness, and resistance to moisture. Polyethylene (PE) is a versatile material with good sealing properties and is often used for heat-sealable confectionery wrappers. These materials cater to different packaging needs in the confectionery industry.</p></p>
