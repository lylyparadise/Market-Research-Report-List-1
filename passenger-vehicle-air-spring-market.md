<p><p>The passenger vehicle air spring market includes three major types: convoluted type, sleeve type, and rolling lobe type. Convoluted type air springs have a bellows-like shape and offer improved ride comfort and load-carrying capacity. Sleeve type air springs consist of flexible sleeves and provide a smoother ride quality. Rolling lobe type air springs have a rolling sleeve that provides good ride comfort and load-carrying capability. These different types cater to varying passenger vehicle needs and preferences, delivering enhanced suspension performance and overall driving experience.</p></p>
