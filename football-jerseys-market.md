<p><p>Football jerseys can be categorized into two main market types: on-field football shirts and player issue football shirts. On-field football shirts are the ones worn by professional football teams during matches and can be purchased by fans. These jerseys are replicas of the ones worn by players and are produced in large quantities for mass sales. On the other hand, player issue football shirts are specifically designed for professional players and are not sold in large quantities to the public. These jerseys often have additional features like improved technology and customizations to meet the specific needs of players.</p></p>
