<p><p>The industrial screening machine market consists of various types of screening machines utilized in industries for various purposes. Fixed screen is a stationary type of screening machine used for separating solid materials based on size. Trommel screen is a cylindrical-shaped screening machine capable of separating materials of different sizes using a rotating drum. Vibrating screen is a type of screening machine that utilizes vibrations to separate solid materials based on their size and shape. These screening machines are essential in industrial processes such as mining, construction, and recycling.</p></p>
