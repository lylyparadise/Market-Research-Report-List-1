<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The vertical mixed flow pump market comprises three types of impellers: semi-open, open, and enclosed. Semi-open impeller pumps have a partially open impeller, allowing some fluid to pass through the vanes. Open impeller pumps have fully open vanes that allow maximum fluid to pass through. Enclosed impeller pumps have a closed impeller, ensuring complete containment of the fluid. These impeller types cater to different applications, with semi-open impellers providing a balance between efficiency and solids handling, open impellers facilitating easy solid passage, and enclosed impellers offering higher efficiency and durability.</p></p>
