<p><p>Temper mills are used in the metal industry to improve the flatness and surface finish of metal sheets or strips. There are different types of temper mills available in the market. The 2HI temper mill is a two-roll mill that provides a simple and cost-effective solution for metal tempering. The 4HI temper mill, as the name suggests, has four rolls and offers more precise control over the tempering process. The 6HI temper mill is similar to the 4HI mill but with an additional intermediate roll, allowing for even higher precision and improved flatness. The "Others" category includes any other types of temper mills not specifically mentioned.</p></p>
