<p><p>The Flame Retardant PP Granules market is divided into two main types - Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. Halogen Type flame retardants contain halogenated compounds such as bromine and chlorine, which release toxic gases when burned. On the other hand, Halogen Free Type flame retardants utilize alternative non-halogenated compounds to inhibit the spread of fire without emitting harmful gases. Halogen Free Type is gaining popularity due to its environmentally friendly nature and increasing concerns about health and safety.</p></p>
