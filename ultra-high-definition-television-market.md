<p><p>The Ultra-High Definition Television (UHDTV) market comprises various types of display technologies such as LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) TVs use a liquid crystal layer in between two polarized glass panels to control light transmission, offering good picture quality and energy efficiency. LED (Light-Emitting Diode) TVs use LED backlighting behind the LCD panel for improved brightness and contrast. OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) TVs use organic compounds that generate light when an electric current is applied, providing superior black levels, high contrast ratios, and wider viewing angles.</p></p>
