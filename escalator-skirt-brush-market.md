<p><p>An escalator skirt brush, also known as an escalator safety brush, is a mechanical component designed to improve passenger safety and prevent accidents on escalators. It is a brush strip that is installed along the sides of escalators, creating a barrier between the moving steps and the stationary skirt panel.</p><p>The global escalator skirt brush market is projected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The market is driven by the increasing emphasis on passenger safety in public places, such as airports, shopping malls, and metro stations. Escalator skirt brushes are essential in reducing the risk of accidents, such as clothing or shoe entrapments, by preventing objects from falling through gaps between the steps and the skirt panel.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the market growth is the rising installation of escalators in urban infrastructure projects worldwide. As urbanization continues to drive the demand for efficient transportation and modern infrastructure, the installation of escalators in various public spaces is becoming increasingly common.
