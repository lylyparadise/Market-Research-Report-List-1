<p><p>In the market for portable remote airborne particle counters, there are two main types: ionizing and non-ionizing. Ionizing particle counters use radiation to ionize particles, making them detectable and countable. These counters are typically used in environments with high concentrations of smaller particles, such as cleanrooms. Non-ionizing particle counters, on the other hand, use light scattering or laser technology to detect and count particles. These counters are commonly used in various industries to monitor air quality and identify potential contaminants.</p></p>
