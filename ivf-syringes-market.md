<p><p>IVF syringes are used in the field of In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) to administer various injections. Intramuscular Injection syringes are designed for injecting medications into muscle tissue, ensuring rapid absorption and distribution. Subcutaneous Injection syringes deliver medications into the fatty layer beneath the skin, allowing gradual absorption. Both types of syringes are essential in IVF procedures to accurately administer medications to patients. Each syringe type is specifically designed to meet the unique requirements of different injection techniques, thereby facilitating the successful implementation of IVF treatment.</p></p>
