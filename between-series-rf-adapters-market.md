<p><p>Between Series RF Adapters refer to connectors that are used to join two different types of RF (radio frequency) connectors together. There are three main types: Female, Male, and Female with O Ring. </p><p>A Female adapter has a socket-like structure that allows a male connector to be inserted into it. Conversely, a Male adapter has a pin-like structure that fits into a female connector. </p><p>A Female with O Ring adapter also has the socket-like structure, but it includes an O ring seal to provide additional protection against moisture or dust. These adapters are commonly used in RF applications to ensure seamless connectivity and signal transmission between different types of connectors.</p></p>
