Force balancer inclination sensors utilize a counterweight system to maintain balance and accurately measure inclination. Electrolytic inclination sensors use electrolyte fluids to gauge the angle. Other types of inclination sensors include capacitive, MEMS, and potentiometric sensors. These sensors find applications in various industries such as construction, aerospace, automotive, and marine for tasks like monitoring stability, tilt control, and leveling systems.</p></p>
