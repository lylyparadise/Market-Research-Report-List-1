<p><p>The Offset Lithography Printing Machine market can be classified into two types: Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press and Web-Fed Offset Printing Press. A Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press is designed to print on individual sheets of paper, making it ideal for small to medium print runs and flexible in terms of paper size and thickness. On the other hand, a Web-Fed Offset Printing Press is designed to print on a continuous roll of paper, allowing for rapid printing of large quantities, making it suitable for high-volume print jobs like newspapers or magazines.</p></p>
