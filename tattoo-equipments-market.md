<p><p>The tattoo equipment market consists of two main types: coil tattoo equipment and rotary tattoo equipment. Coil tattoo equipment uses electromagnetic coils to power the tattoo machine, creating a strong and consistent needle motion. On the other hand, rotary tattoo equipment operates with a rotating motor, resulting in a smoother and quieter tattooing process. Both types have their own advantages and are favored by different tattoo artists based on their personal preferences and tattooing styles.</p></p>
