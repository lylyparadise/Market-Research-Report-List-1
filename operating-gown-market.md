<p><p>The operating gown market comprises two main types: disposable and non-disposable gowns. Disposable gowns are designed for single use and are made from materials such as polypropylene, ensuring optimal hygiene standards by preventing cross-contamination. These gowns are widely used in healthcare facilities and surgical settings to protect both healthcare providers and patients. On the other hand, non-disposable gowns are made from materials like cotton or polyester and are reusable after proper sanitization. Non-disposable gowns are commonly used in settings where frequent gown changes are not required, such as dental clinics or outpatient facilities.</p></p>
