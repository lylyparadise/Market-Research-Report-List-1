<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The electronic filter market is composed of various types of filters used in electronic circuits. Low-pass filters allow low-frequency signals to pass through while attenuating high-frequency signals. High-pass filters do the opposite, allowing high-frequency signals to pass while attenuating low-frequency signals. Band-pass filters allow a specific range of frequencies to pass through, while attenuating frequencies outside that range. Band-stop filters, also known as notch filters, attenuate a specific range of frequencies while allowing others to pass through. All-pass filters allow all frequencies to pass through, but they alter the phase relationship between the input and output signals.</p></p>
