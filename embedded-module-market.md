<p><p>The embedded module market consists of two main types: hardware and software. In the hardware market, companies design and manufacture physical components that are integrated into other devices or systems. These hardware modules typically include processors, memory, and communication interfaces. On the other hand, the software market focuses on developing the programs and firmware that run on these hardware modules. This software enables the functionality and control of embedded systems, allowing them to perform specific tasks or applications. Both hardware and software modules are essential for creating and operating embedded systems effectively.</p></p>
