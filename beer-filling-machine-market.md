<p><strong>The Beer Filling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Type</li><li>Automatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beer filling machines are categorized into two types: manual type and automatic type. Manual type machines require human intervention to operate the filling process, such as manually placing bottles, filling them with beer, and capping them. These machines are suitable for small-scale operations with limited production capacity. On the other hand, automatic type machines are equipped with advanced technology and can perform the entire filling process autonomously. They can handle large-scale production efficiently by automatically feeding bottles, filling them accurately, and sealing them without human assistance.</p></p>
