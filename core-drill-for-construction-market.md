<p><ul><li>Dry Core Drill</li><li>Wet Core Drill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core drills for construction come in two main types: dry core drills and wet core drills. Dry core drills are used without water and typically have diamond-tipped blades for drilling through materials such as concrete, brick, and stone. Wet core drills, on the other hand, require water to cool the blades and flush out debris during drilling. They are commonly used for larger-scale projects and produce cleaner cuts. Both types of core drills are essential tools in the construction industry for creating precise and efficient holes.</p></p>
