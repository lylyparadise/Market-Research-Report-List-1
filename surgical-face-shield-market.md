<p><p>The Surgical Face Shield market can be divided into three main types: Full Length Face Shield, 3/4 Length Face Shield, and Half Length Face Shield. A Full Length Face Shield covers the entire face, providing maximum protection against droplets and splashes. A 3/4 Length Face Shield covers most of the face, excluding the chin area. It offers good protection while allowing more freedom of movement. A Half Length Face Shield covers only the upper part of the face, leaving the lower face exposed. It provides limited protection but allows for better communication and breathability.</p></p>
