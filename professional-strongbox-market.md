<p><p>The professional strongbox market consists of two main types of safes: mechanical safe and electric safe. Mechanical safes are operated by manual mechanisms such as combination locks or key locks. These safes do not require any external power source to function. On the other hand, electric safes use electronic components like a digital keypad or biometric scanner and require electricity to operate. Both types offer secure storage solutions for professionals, but with different security mechanisms to cater to individual preferences and requirements.</p></p>
