<p><p>Heartbeat abnormality detection equipment market includes various types of devices used for monitoring and detecting abnormal heart rhythms. Holter monitor is a portable device that records the heart's electrical activity over a period of time. Mobile cardiac telemetry utilizes a wearable sensor that continuously monitors and transmits ECG data to healthcare professionals remotely. Implantable heart monitors are devices that are surgically placed inside the patient's chest to monitor heart rhythms. ECG (electrocardiogram) is a non-invasive diagnostic tool that records the heart's electrical activity. Other types of equipment include event monitors and patch monitors that are used for short-term monitoring of heart rhythms.</p></p>
