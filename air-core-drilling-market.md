<p><strong>The Air Core Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dust Drilling</li><li>Mist Drilling</li><li>Foam Drilling</li><li>Aerated Fluid Drilling</li><li>Nitrogen Membrane Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air core drilling is a drilling method that uses compressed air to lift cuttings out of the borehole. There are various types of air core drilling techniques that can be employed. Dust drilling involves using compressed air alone to remove the cuttings from the hole. Mist drilling uses a misting agent to aid in cuttings removal. Foam drilling utilizes a foam agent to facilitate efficient drilling and cuttings removal. Aerated fluid drilling involves incorporating air or gas into the drilling fluid. Nitrogen membrane drilling employs a nitrogen membrane system to remove the cuttings from the drill string.</p></p>
