<p><p>A grill pan is a type of cookware used to simulate the cooking effect of grilling, with raised ridges or grill lines on its surface. These ridges create char marks on food, giving it a more grilled-like appearance and flavor. Grill pans are typically made of materials such as cast iron, stainless steel, or aluminum, and they are commonly used for cooking indoors on stovetops.</p><p>The future outlook of the grill pans market is expected to be positive, with a predicted growth rate of 8% during the forecasted period. There are several factors contributing to this growth. Firstly, there is a rising trend of indoor grilling among consumers who want to enjoy the taste of grilled food without the need for an outdoor grill. Grill pans provide a convenient and versatile option for such individuals.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing popularity of healthy cooking methods is driving the demand for grill pans. The ridged surface of the pan allows excess fats and oils to drain away from the food, making it a healthier cooking option. This attribute is particularly attractive to health-conscious consumers who are increasingly looking for ways to reduce their fat intake.</p><p>Furthermore, the grill pans market is benefitting from the growing trend of home cooking.
