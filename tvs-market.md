<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uni-polar TVS and bi-polar TVS are both market types in the TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against voltage spikes in one direction only, typically positive or negative. They are commonly used in applications where voltage transients occur in only one polarity, such as in automotive electronics. On the other hand, bi-polar TVS devices offer protection against voltage spikes in both positive and negative directions, making them suitable for applications with bidirectional voltage transients, like telecommunications and industrial equipment.</p></p>
