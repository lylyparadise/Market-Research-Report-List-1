<p><p>Oral nicotine pouches are tobacco-free, smokeless products that contain nicotine and are placed in the mouth between the lip and gum. One major factor driving the market growth is the increasing global awareness of the health risks associated with traditional tobacco use. With concerns over smoking-related diseases and the desire to quit smoking, many individuals are seeking alternatives that can deliver nicotine without the harmful effects of combustion. Oral nicotine pouches offer a smoke-free and tobacco-free option, making them an appealing choice for consumers.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing steady growth due to the rising adoption of smokeless nicotine products among the younger generation.
