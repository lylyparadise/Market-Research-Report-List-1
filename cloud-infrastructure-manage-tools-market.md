<p><ul><li>Software-As-A-Service (SaaS)</li><li>Platform-As-A-Service (PaaS)</li><li>Infrastructure-As-A-Service (IaaS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud Infrastructure Management Tools market includes three main types: </p><p>1. Software-As-A-Service (SaaS) solutions provide cloud-based software applications that are accessible via the internet. These tools allow users to manage and control their cloud infrastructure without having to worry about hardware or software installation and maintenance.</p><p>2. Platform-As-A-Service (PaaS) solutions offer a complete development and deployment environment in the cloud. Users can build, test, and deploy applications without needing to manage the underlying infrastructure. PaaS tools provide a streamlined platform for developers to focus on coding and application development.</p><p>3. Infrastructure-As-A-Service (IaaS) solutions provide virtualized computing resources over the internet. Users can access and manage virtual machines, networks, storage, and other infrastructure components. IaaS offers flexibility and scalability, allowing businesses to easily adjust their infrastructure needs as their requirements evolve.</p><p>These market types enable organizations to efficiently manage their cloud infrastructure without the burden of physical hardware and maintenance, providing flexibility, scalability, and streamlining development processes.</p></p>
