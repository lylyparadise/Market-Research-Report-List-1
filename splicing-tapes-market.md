<p><ul><li>Repulpable</li><li>Non-Repulpable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Splicing tapes are used in the paper, printing, and packaging industries to join two rolls of material together during production. Repulpable splicing tapes are designed to dissolve during the recycling process, making them environmentally friendly. Non-repulpable splicing tapes, on the other hand, do not dissolve and are better suited for applications where recycling is not a priority. Both types of splicing tapes play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient production processes in various industries.</p></p>
