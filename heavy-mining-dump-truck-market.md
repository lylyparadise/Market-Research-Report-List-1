<p><ul><li>Articulated Trucks</li><li>Rigid Trucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy mining dump truck market can be divided into two types: articulated trucks and rigid trucks. Articulated trucks are designed with a hinge between the cab and the dump bed, allowing for greater maneuverability in tight spaces. They are ideal for operations requiring frequent turning and navigating uneven terrain. On the other hand, rigid trucks have a single, rigid chassis, providing stability and strength for hauling heavy loads over longer distances. Both types cater to the specific needs of the mining industry and are crucial for efficient transportation of materials.</p></p>
