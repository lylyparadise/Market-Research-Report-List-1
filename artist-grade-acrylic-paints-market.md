<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The artist grade acrylic paints market can be classified into two main types: fluid acrylics and heavy-body acrylics. Fluid acrylics are characterized by their smooth consistency and are ideal for techniques like pouring, glazing, and watercolor effects. They have a high pigment load, are highly transparent, and dry quickly. On the other hand, heavy-body acrylics have a thicker consistency and are suitable for impasto techniques, as they retain brush or knife marks. They have a dense pigment load, provide excellent coverage, and have a longer drying time.</p></p>
