<p><p>The Aircraft Ovens market includes various types of ovens used in aircraft catering. Electric ovens are powered by electricity and heat the food by passing electric current through heating elements. Conduction ovens use direct heat transfer through a solid medium, like a metal plate, to cook the food. Convection ovens circulate hot air through the cooking chamber, ensuring even and faster cooking. Other types of ovens in this market may include microwave ovens or combination ovens that combine different cooking methods for more versatile food preparation in-flight.</p></p>
