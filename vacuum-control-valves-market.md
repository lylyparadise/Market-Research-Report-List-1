<p><p>The vacuum control valves market consists of two types: manual vacuum control valves and electronic vacuum control valves. Manual vacuum control valves are operated manually using a handheld lever or wheel to control the vacuum flow. On the other hand, electronic vacuum control valves are automated devices that use electronic signals to regulate the vacuum flow. These valves are controlled remotely through a control system or programmable logic controller. Both types play a crucial role in controlling the vacuum flow in various industrial applications.</p></p>
