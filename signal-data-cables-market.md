<p><p>The Signal & Data Cables market comprises three main types: Twisted Pair (Copper), Coax (Copper), and Optic Cables (Fibre). Twisted Pair cables are made of pairs of copper wires twisted together, offering cost-effectiveness and flexibility in data transmission. Coaxial cables, also copper-based, consist of a central conductor surrounded by insulating layers, ensuring signal integrity in high-frequency applications. Optic Cables use fiber optics to transmit data as pulses of light. Their superior bandwidth, longer transmission distances, and immunity to electromagnetic interference make them ideal for high-speed and long-distance data transmission.</p></p>
