<p><p>A router is a networking device that forwards data packets between computer networks. It acts as a mediator between devices on a local network (LAN) and devices on other networks, such as the internet. It makes decisions about the best path for data to travel based on the destination IP address. On the other hand, a switch is a device that connects devices within a local network. It receives and transmits data packets within the network, facilitating communication between devices.</p><p>The router and switch market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for high-speed internet connections, rising data traffic, and the growing adoption of IoT devices. Additionally, the surge in cloud-based applications and services is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its landscape. One significant trend is the growing adoption of software-defined networking (SDN) and network functions virtualization (NFV) technologies. SDN and NFV offer flexibility, scalability, and cost savings, driving their adoption in enterprise networks.</p><p>Another trend is the rapid advancement in wireless technologies. With the increasing popularity of wireless devices, there is a rising need for routers and switches that can support higher data speeds and provide reliable wireless connectivity.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a shift towards intelligent and programmable networking solutions. The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies into routers and switches enables automation, self-healing, and improved network performance.</p><p>Overall, the router and switch market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.20% during the forecast period.
