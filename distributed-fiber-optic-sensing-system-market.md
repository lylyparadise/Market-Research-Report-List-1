DFOS systems enable real-time monitoring, detect leakages and intrusions, and ensure the integrity of infrastructure.</p><p>In the power and utility sector, DFOS systems are used to monitor and manage transmission and distribution lines, substations, and power cables. It helps in preventing electrical failures, improving efficiency, and reducing maintenance costs.</p><p>The civil engineering sector is also a major consumer of DFOS systems for monitoring the structural health of buildings, bridges, tunnels, and dams. The transportation sector utilizes DFOS systems for monitoring railways, highways, and airports to ensure safety and optimize operations.</p><p>The latest trends in the DFOS market include the development of advanced sensing technologies for higher accuracy and sensitivity, integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies for real-time data analysis, and the adoption of distributed acoustic sensing for perimeter security applications.</p><p>In conclusion, the DFOS market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period.
