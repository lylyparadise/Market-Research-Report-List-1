<p><p>The niche perfume market comprises various types, including water-based perfumes and alcohol-based perfumes. Water-based perfumes are created by blending fragrance oils with water, resulting in a lighter and more subtle scent. They are ideal for individuals who prefer a less intense fragrance. On the other hand, alcohol-based perfumes are made by combining fragrance oils with alcohol, which helps disperse the scent and allows it to last longer on the skin. These perfumes are often preferred by those who desire a stronger and more long-lasting fragrance.</p></p>
