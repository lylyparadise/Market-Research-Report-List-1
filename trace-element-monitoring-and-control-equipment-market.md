<p><p>Trace element monitoring and control equipment market types include Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy and Electrochemical Analysis. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy is a technique that measures the concentration of elements in a sample by measuring the absorption of light at specific wavelengths. Electrochemical Analysis involves using electrodes to measure the electrical properties of a sample, allowing for the detection of trace elements. Both of these methods are commonly used in industries such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and food safety to ensure quality control and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
