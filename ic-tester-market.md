<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC Tester market can be divided into two main types: Wafer Tester and Packaged Device Tester. Wafer Tester is used to test integrated circuits at the wafer level before they are cut into individual chips. This allows for early detection of defects and reduces overall manufacturing costs. Packaged Device Tester, on the other hand, is used to test individual ICs after they have been packaged. This ensures that the final product meets quality standards before being shipped to customers. Both types of testers play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and functionality of integrated circuits.</p></p>
