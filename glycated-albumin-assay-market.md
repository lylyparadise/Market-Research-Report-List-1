<p><p>The Glycated Albumin Assay is a diagnostic test used to measure the level of glycated albumin in the blood. Glycated albumin is a form of protein that is formed when glucose binds to albumin in the bloodstream. This test is mainly used to monitor and manage diabetes, as glycated albumin provides a more accurate measurement of average blood glucose levels over a shorter period compared to hemoglobin A1c (HbA1c) tests.</p><p>The market for Glycated Albumin Assay is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.50% during the forecast period.
