<p><strong>The Mud Guns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Mud Gun</li><li>Rotary Mud Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the mud guns market, there are two main types: fixed mud guns and rotary mud guns. Fixed mud guns are stationary and are typically used in drilling mud tanks to agitate the fluid and prevent solids from settling. On the other hand, rotary mud guns are attached to a rotating pipe or swivel assembly and are used to clean the wellbore by spraying high-pressure mud onto the walls. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining proper fluid circulation and wellbore cleaning during drilling operations.</p></p>
