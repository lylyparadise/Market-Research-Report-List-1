<p><p>The Patient Monitoring Simulator System market includes two types: Standard Vital Signs and Customizable Vital Signs. Standard Vital Signs offer a predetermined set of vital sign parameters commonly used in patient monitoring, such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation. Customizable Vital Signs, on the other hand, allow healthcare professionals to modify and adjust the vital sign parameters based on specific patient needs or training scenarios. Both types serve the purpose of simulating realistic patient conditions for training healthcare providers without risk to actual patients.</p></p>
