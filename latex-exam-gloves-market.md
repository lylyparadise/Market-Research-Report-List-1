<p><p>The Latex Exam Gloves market is divided into two main types: sterile gloves and non-sterile gloves. They undergo rigorous sterilization processes and are individually packaged to maintain their sterility. On the other hand, non-sterile gloves are commonly used in non-medical settings or tasks that do not require a sterile environment, like routine check-ups or laboratory work.
