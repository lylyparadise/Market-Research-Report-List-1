<p><p>The piping components market is categorized into two main types: metal pipe components and plastic pipe components. Metal pipe components include fittings, valves, flanges, and couplings that are made of various metals such as steel, copper, and iron. These components are preferred in applications that require high strength, durability, and resistance to extreme conditions. On the other hand, plastic pipe components, including fittings, valves, and connectors, are made of different types of plastic materials such as PVC, HDPE, and PPR. They are commonly used in residential and commercial plumbing systems due to their lightweight, corrosion resistance, and easy installation properties.</p></p>
