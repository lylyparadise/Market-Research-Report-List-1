<p><strong>The Screen Changers for Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discontinuous Screen Changers</li><li>Continuous Screen Changers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen changers for extruder are devices used in the plastics industry to filter impurities from the molten plastic during the extrusion process. Discontinuous screen changers operate by stopping the extrusion process to replace the dirty screen with a clean one. Continuous screen changers, on the other hand, allow for a continuous flow of material by utilizing two screens that alternate their cleaning cycles. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the quality and efficiency of extruded plastic products.</p></p>
