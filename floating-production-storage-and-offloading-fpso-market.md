<p><strong>The Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spread Mooring</li><li>Single Point Mooring (SPM)</li><li>Dynamic Positioning (DP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO) is a type of vessel used in offshore oil and gas exploration and production. Market types for FPSOs include Spread Mooring, Single Point Mooring (SPM), and Dynamic Positioning (DP). Spread Mooring involves anchoring the FPSO to multiple points on the seabed using chains or cables. SPM involves mooring the FPSO to a single point using a turret system. DP uses thrusters to maintain the position of the FPSO without the need for mooring. These market types determine the specific technology and equipment used for the FPSO's stability and mobility.</p></p>
