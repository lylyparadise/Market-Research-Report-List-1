<p><p>The Smart Waste Monitoring market includes various types of technologies used for waste monitoring. Ultrasound technology utilizes sound waves to measure the waste level in containers. Infrared technology uses infrared sensors to detect the waste level based on the reflected light. Other types of technologies used in this market include radio frequency identification (RFID) and ultrasonic imaging. These technologies help in efficient waste management by providing real-time data on waste levels, enabling timely collection and reducing overflow or littering issues.</p></p>
