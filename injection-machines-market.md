The market for injection machines can be classified into three types: vertical, horizontal, and other. Vertical type injection machines have a vertical clamping unit and are suitable for applications with a smaller footprint. Horizontal type injection machines have a horizontal clamping unit and are preferred for applications requiring higher production output. The "other" market includes specialized injection machines used for specific applications like multi-color or multi-material injection molding. These machines expand the versatility and capabilities of injection molding for various industries.</p></p>
