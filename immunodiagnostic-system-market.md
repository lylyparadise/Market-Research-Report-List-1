<p><p>Immunodiagnostic systems are used to detect and analyze antigens or antibodies in biological samples. Chemiluminescence analyzers are a type of immunodiagnostic system that use chemical reactions to produce light signals for detection. Microtiter plate analyzers utilize specially designed plates with multiple wells to simultaneously test multiple samples. Other types of immunodiagnostic systems could include enzyme immunoassay analyzers, radioimmunoassay analyzers, or fluorescence analyzers. These systems are essential tools in medical laboratories, enabling the accurate and efficient diagnosis of various diseases and conditions through the detection of specific antibodies or antigens.</p></p>
