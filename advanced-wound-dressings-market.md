<p><p>Advanced wound dressings are innovative products used for the treatment of chronic wounds. Foam dressings provide a moist environment and absorb excess fluid. Hydrocolloids are self-adhesive dressings that form a gel on contact with wound fluid, creating a protective barrier. Alginates are made from seaweed and offer high absorbency, promoting wound healing. Transparent film dressings provide a waterproof layer while allowing wound monitoring. Hydrofiber dressings form a gel when exposed to exudate, helping with wound healing. Hydrogels provide moisture to the wound bed. Collagen dressings stimulate tissue regeneration. Other types include silicone and antimicrobial dressings.</p></p>
