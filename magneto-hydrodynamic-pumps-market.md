<p><p>Magneto hydrodynamic pumps are devices that use magnetic fields to generate fluid flow without the need for any mechanical parts. There are two types of these pumps: permanent magnet type and magnetic coil type. </p><p>Permanent magnet type hydrodynamic pumps utilize a fixed magnet to generate a magnetic field, which interacts with the conductive fluid to induce a current and produce a thrust force for pumping. </p><p>On the other hand, magnetic coil type hydrodynamic pumps utilize a coil wound around the pump body, which, when energized with an electrical current, generates a magnetic field that interacts with the fluid to induce a current and create the necessary thrust for pumping.</p></p>
