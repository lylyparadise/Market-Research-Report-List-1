<p><p>The Telecom Services for Call Centers Software market is classified into two types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises software is installed locally on the call center's servers and managed by their own IT team. It offers high security and customization options but requires significant upfront investment and maintenance costs. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is hosted on the provider's servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs, but data security and customization options might be limited depending on the provider.</p></p>
