These systems can be categorized into three main types: Non-magnetic Pump Systems, Magnetic Pump Systems with Shielding, and Tubing and Disposables. Non-magnetic Pump Systems use non-ferrous materials to avoid interference with the MRI machine. Magnetic Pump Systems with Shielding have special shielding to protect against magnetic field effects. Tubing and Disposables include non-magnetic tubing and disposable components to ensure patient safety during MRI scans.</p></p>
