<p><strong>The Stormwater Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screening Equipment</li><li>Separation Equipment</li><li>Filtration Equipment</li><li>Infiltration Equipment</li><li>Retention Equipment</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stormwater treatment equipment market types include screening equipment, which mechanically removes large debris and trash from stormwater runoff. Separation equipment utilizes gravitational forces to separate pollutants (such as oil and sediment) from the stormwater. Filtration equipment employs various filter media to capture and remove contaminants from the water. Infiltration equipment allows stormwater to slowly infiltrate into the ground, where it can be naturally filtered and purified. Retention equipment temporarily stores stormwater for future use or controlled release. Other market types may include advanced treatment technologies like biofiltration systems or chemical treatments.</p></p>
