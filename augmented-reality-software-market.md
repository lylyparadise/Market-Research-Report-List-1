<p><p>The augmented reality software market consists of two types, namely cloud-based and on-premises market. Cloud-based augmented reality software refers to software that is hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet. This type of software offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for on-site infrastructure. On the other hand, on-premises augmented reality software is installed and operated on the user's local hardware and infrastructure. This type of software provides more control and privacy but requires higher initial investment and maintenance.</p></p>
