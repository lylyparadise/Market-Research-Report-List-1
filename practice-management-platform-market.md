<p><p>The practice management platform market is divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based platforms operate through remote servers, allowing users to access and manage data online. This type offers flexibility, automatic updates, and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises platforms are installed locally on individual computers or servers, where data is stored and managed internally. While this type provides data control and security, it requires maintenance and may lack the convenience of remote access and automatic updates.</p></p>
