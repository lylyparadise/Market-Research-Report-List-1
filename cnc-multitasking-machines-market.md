<p><p>CNC (Computer Numerical Control) multitasking machines are advanced manufacturing systems that combine multiple tools and functions into a single machine. Manufacturers are increasingly focusing on developing hybrid machines that combine additive manufacturing (3D printing) capabilities with traditional subtractive machining processes.
