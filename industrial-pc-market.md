They are compact, space-saving, and provide real-time monitoring and control capabilities.</p><p>Rackmount IPC refers to industrial PCs that are designed to be mounted on standard 19-inch server racks. They are usually used in data centers or server rooms and offer high computing power and storage capacity.</p><p>Box IPC, also called box PCs, are industrial PCs that come in a standalone box-like form factor. They are versatile and can be used in various applications such as factory automation, transportation, or surveillance.</p><p>Others in the industrial PC market include specialized types like DIN rail IPCs or custom-built PCs tailored to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
