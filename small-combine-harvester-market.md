<p><p>The Small Combine Harvester market consists of various types of harvesters. Self-propelled Crawler Type Full Feed Combine Harvesters have tracks for better maneuverability in challenging terrains. Self-propelled Crawler Semi-feed Combine Harvesters have a narrower inlet throat and are compatible with smaller crops. Suspended Combine Harvesters are suspended from the tractor, allowing for better harvesting performance. These variations cater to different needs and conditions in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
