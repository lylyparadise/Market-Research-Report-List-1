<p><p>The Side Milling Cutter market consists of three main types: Full Side Milling Cutter, Half-Side Milling Cutter, and Stagger-Tooth Side Milling Cutter. A Full Side Milling Cutter is designed with teeth on both sides, allowing it to cut a wider range of materials. A Half-Side Milling Cutter only has teeth on one side, making it suitable for edge or slot cutting. A Stagger-Tooth Side Milling Cutter has teeth arranged in a staggered pattern, enabling smoother cutting and reducing vibration. These variations cater to different cutting needs in various industries.</p></p>
