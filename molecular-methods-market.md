Immunoassay is used to detect and quantify specific proteins or antibodies. Biosensors provide real-time detection by utilizing biological recognition elements. Microarray allows simultaneous analysis of multiple genes or proteins. Other molecular methods include techniques like sequencing, cloning, and gene expression analysis.</p></p>
