<p><p>The Waste Heat Recovery System (WHRS) market consists of two main types, namely Steam Rankine Cycle (SRC) and Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC). SRC utilizes steam generated by waste heat to produce electricity through a turbine. ORC, on the other hand, employs organic fluids with low boiling points, using them to generate power when heated by waste heat. Both systems capture and exploit waste heat from industrial processes to increase overall energy efficiency and reduce carbon emissions, offering sustainable and cost-effective solutions for various industries.</p></p>
