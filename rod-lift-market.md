There are two primary types of rod lift markets: the beam market and the non-beam market. In the beam market, the traditional beam pumping units are used, which consist of a central walking beam connected to a polished rod and sucker rod string. On the other hand, the non-beam market utilizes alternative forms of lifting equipment, such as hydraulic pumping units or electric submersible pumps, which offer different advantages and capabilities for efficient production.</p></p>
