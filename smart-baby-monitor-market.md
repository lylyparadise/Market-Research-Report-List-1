<p><p>The Smart Baby Monitor market encompasses three main types: audio baby monitors, video baby monitors, and internet baby monitors. Audio baby monitors transmit sound from the baby's room to the parent unit, enabling parents to listen to their baby remotely. Video baby monitors provide live video feed along with audio, allowing parents to see and hear their baby. Internet baby monitors connect to the internet, enabling parents to access the live feed remotely through a smartphone or computer. These types of monitors provide convenience and peace of mind for parents by ensuring continuous monitoring of their child.</p></p>
