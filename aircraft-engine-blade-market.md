<p><p>The aircraft engine blade market includes various types of blades such as compressor blades, turbine blades, and fan blades. Compressor blades are responsible for compressing air in the engine for combustion. Turbine blades extract energy from the high-temperature, high-pressure gases produced through combustion to power the aircraft. Fan blades, located at the front of the engine, draw in and push a large amount of air to provide thrust. These different types of blades work together to ensure efficient and reliable aircraft engine performance.</p></p>
