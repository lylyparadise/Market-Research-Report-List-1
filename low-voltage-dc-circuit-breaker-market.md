<p><p>The Low Voltage DC Circuit Breaker market consists of two major types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB) and Molded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB). MCBs are compact devices designed for residential and commercial applications, providing protection against overload and short circuits. They typically have lower current ratings and are used in smaller electrical systems. On the other hand, MCCBs are larger and more robust circuit breakers commonly used in industrial settings. They have higher current ratings and provide enhanced protection against major electrical faults. Both types serve the purpose of interrupting the current flow in low voltage DC circuits when necessary to prevent damage or hazards.</p></p>
