<p><ul><li>Cold Isostatic Pressing Machine</li><li>Hot Isostatic Pressing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isostatic pressing machines market includes two major types: cold isostatic pressing machines and hot isostatic pressing machines. Cold isostatic pressing machines apply uniform pressure on materials, usually ceramics or composites, to produce shaped products. They are used for applications like manufacturing electronic components and fuel cells. On the other hand, hot isostatic pressing machines are utilized to treat and consolidate materials at high temperatures and pressure. This process helps improve the mechanical properties of materials like metals and alloys, and find applications in aerospace, automotive, and energy sectors.</p></p>
