<p><strong>The Preclinical MRI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment</li><li>Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The preclinical MRI equipment market can be divided into two types: permanent magnet preclinical MRI equipment and superconductive preclinical MRI equipment. Permanent magnet preclinical MRI equipment utilizes a stationary magnet, typically made of a material like iron, to generate a magnetic field. It is cost-effective and suitable for small animal imaging. On the other hand, superconductive preclinical MRI equipment uses powerful superconducting magnets cooled to extremely low temperatures to produce a strong and stable magnetic field. It is highly sensitive and provides high-resolution imaging, making it ideal for advanced research applications.</p></p>
