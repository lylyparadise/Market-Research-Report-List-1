<p><strong>The Elevator Door-motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inverter Door-motor</li><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator door motor market includes two major types: Inverter Door-motor and Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motor. An inverter door-motor operates through an inverter, which converts DC power from the elevator's control system into AC power to control the motor speed. On the other hand, a permanent magnet synchronous door-motor uses permanent magnets in the rotor, which helps achieve higher efficiency and improved control over the motor. Both types of door-motor play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and reliable elevator door operations.</p></p>
