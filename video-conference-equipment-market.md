<p><p>The video conference equipment market can be classified into three types: on-premise, cloud-based, and hybrid. On-premise systems involve installing and maintaining hardware and software within a company's infrastructure, offering high security and control. Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, rely on external servers and require an internet connection for communication, offering flexibility and scalability. Hybrid systems combine elements of both on-premise and cloud-based solutions, allowing companies to leverage the benefits of both approaches. These classifications determine where the video conference equipment is hosted and how it is accessed.</p></p>
