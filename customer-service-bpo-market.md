<p><p>Customer service BPO, or Business Process Outsourcing, refers to the practice of contracting customer service operations to external service providers. Onshore outsourcing refers to the practice of outsourcing these services to service providers within the same country, ensuring that customer service agents are located in the same country as the customers they serve. Offshore outsourcing, on the other hand, involves contracting customer service operations to foreign service providers, where customer service agents may be located in a different country. Both onshore and offshore outsourcing offer companies the opportunity to reduce costs and access specialized expertise.</p></p>
