<p><p>The Dental Zirconia Material Market includes two main types: Zirconia Disc and Zirconia Block. Zirconia Discs are flat circular disks used for manufacturing dental restorations such as crowns or bridges. They are available in various sizes and thicknesses to meet different clinical requirements. On the other hand, Zirconia Blocks are solid rectangular blocks used to create dental restorations through computer-aided design and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD/CAM) systems. These blocks are milled to shape the desired restoration, offering high precision and aesthetics in dental prosthetics.</p></p>
