<p><p>Stadium seating can be divided into two types: foldable and non-foldable. Foldable seating refers to chairs or benches that can be easily folded and stored when not in use, allowing for flexibility and efficient space utilization. On the other hand, non-foldable seating refers to fixed chairs or benches that are permanently installed in the stadium, providing a more stable and durable seating option. Both types of seating serve the purpose of accommodating large crowds in stadiums but offer different advantages in terms of convenience and long-term usability.</p></p>
