<p><p>Instrument transformers are devices commonly used in electrical power systems to measure and protect electrical equipment. There are two main types: current transformers (CTs) and voltage transformers (VTs). A current transformer steps down the high current flowing through a circuit to a level suitable for measurement, while a voltage transformer steps down the high voltage to a safe and measurable level. Both CTs and VTs play a crucial role in accurate measurement, protection, and control of electrical systems, making them essential components in the power industry.</p></p>
