<p><p>Submersible Unmanned Vehicles (SUVs) include Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are remotely controlled by operators on the surface through a tether, allowing them to explore deep underwater environments for commercial, scientific, or military purposes. On the other hand, AUVs operate autonomously without any human intervention, using pre-programmed instructions to gather data, map the ocean floor, or perform specific tasks. Both ROVs and AUVs play significant roles in underwater exploration, research, and various industries such as oil and gas, defense, marine science, and offshore infrastructure inspection.</p></p>
