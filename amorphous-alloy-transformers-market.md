<p><p>Amorphous Alloy Transformers are used to transfer electrical energy between different voltage levels. The market for these transformers can be segmented into two types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed Amorphous Alloy Transformers use oil as a cooling medium to dissipate heat generated during operation. On the other hand, Dry-type Amorphous Alloy Transformers use air as a cooling medium, eliminating the need for oil. Both types offer advantages such as energy efficiency and reduced losses, making them suitable for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
