<p><p>The online contact management software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as businesses do not need to invest in hardware or maintain software. On the other hand, on-premises contact management software is installed and maintained on the company's own servers.
