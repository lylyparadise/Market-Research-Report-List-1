<p><p>Body Composition Analyzer Scales market encompasses various types such as Bio-Impedance Analyzers, Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA), Air Displacement Plethysmography, and others. Bio-Impedance Analyzers measure resistance to electrical flow within the body to estimate body composition. DEXA uses low-dose X-rays to measure fat, lean, and bone mass. Air Displacement Plethysmography measures body volume using air displacement. Other methods include infrared interactance and ultrasound measurements. These scales are used in fitness centers, hospitals, and homes to provide valuable data for monitoring health, assessing body fat percentage, and tracking progress in weight management programs.</p></p>
