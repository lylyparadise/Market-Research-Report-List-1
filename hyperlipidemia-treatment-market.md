<p><strong>The Hyperlipidemia Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Familial Hyperlipidemia</li><li>Acquired Hyperlipidemia</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hyperlipidemia treatment market includes two main types, namely Familial Hyperlipidemia and Acquired Hyperlipidemia. Familial Hyperlipidemia refers to the condition where individuals inherit abnormal lipid levels from their parents, leading to high cholesterol and triglyceride levels. This type of hyperlipidemia typically requires long-term management through lifestyle changes, medication, and regular monitoring to reduce the risk of cardiovascular diseases. On the other hand, Acquired Hyperlipidemia occurs due to external factors such as poor diet, obesity, sedentary lifestyle, and certain medications. Treating acquired hyperlipidemia involves addressing the underlying cause, adopting healthier habits, and sometimes, medication to manage lipid levels effectively.</p></p>
