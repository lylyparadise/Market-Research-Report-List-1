<p><p>B-type diagnostic ultrasound devices are used in medical imaging to visualize internal organs and structures. These devices have different market types based on their scanning techniques. Fanshaped scanning refers to ultrasound beams that are emitted in a fan-shaped pattern. Linear scanning involves a linear transducer that is moved along the body surface. Mixed type market refers to devices that combine fanshaped and linear scanning techniques. These different scanning techniques allow for various applications and enable healthcare professionals to obtain accurate and comprehensive diagnostic information.</p></p>
