<p><p>The Portable UV Curing System market consists of two types: Spot-Type and Line-Type. Spot-Type systems emit UV light from a centralized spot, allowing targeted curing of small areas or objects. These systems are commonly used for electronics, jewelry, or dental applications. On the other hand, Line-Type systems emit a continuous UV light beam in a straight line, ideal for curing large surfaces or objects. These systems are commonly used in industries such as automotive manufacturing, printing, or furniture production. Both types of systems offer portable solutions for UV curing applications.</p></p>
