<p><p>The automatic transmission oil market caters to various transmission systems, including AT (automatic transmission), AMT (automated manual transmission), DSG (direct-shift gearbox), and CVT (continuously variable transmission). AT is used in traditional automatic transmissions for smooth gear shifting. AMT combines manual and automatic transmission features, providing efficiency and convenience. DSG is a dual-clutch transmission offering fast gear changes and improved fuel efficiency. CVT ensures seamless and stepless gear shifts for better fuel economy. These transmission oils play a crucial role in lubricating, cooling, and protecting the transmission system's components, enhancing overall performance and longevity.</p></p>
