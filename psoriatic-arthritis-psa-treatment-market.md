<p><p>Psoriatic Arthritis (PsA) Treatment Market includes three main types of medications: </p><p>1. Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs): These drugs help reduce pain, inflammation, and stiffness in the joints. They do not slow down the progression of the disease but provide temporary relief from symptoms.</p><p>2. Disease Modifying Antirheumatic Drugs (DMARDs): DMARDs slow down the progression of PsA by targeting the immune system and reducing inflammation. They help prevent joint damage and improve long-term outcomes.</p><p>3. Biologics: Biologic medications are a type of DMARDs that are derived from living cells. They specifically target molecules in the immune system to reduce inflammation and slow down joint damage. They are often used when other treatments fail to provide relief.</p></p>
