<p><p>The acute ischemic stroke therapeutics market consists of various types of medications used for treating stroke. Thrombolytic therapeutics are drugs that dissolve blood clots to restore blood flow to the brain. Antihypertensive therapeutics help lower high blood pressure, reducing the risk of stroke. Antiplatelet therapeutics prevent blood clot formation by inhibiting platelet aggregation. Anticoagulants also prevent blood clotting, but they work by interfering with the blood clotting cascade. All these medications play a crucial role in managing acute ischemic stroke and preventing further damage to the brain.</p></p>
