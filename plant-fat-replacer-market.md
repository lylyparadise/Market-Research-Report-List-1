<p><p>The Plant Fat Replacer market consists of three main types: carbohydrate-based, protein-based, and lipid-based. Carbohydrate-based fat replacers are derived from plant sources and mimic the texture and mouthfeel of fats in food products. Protein-based fat replacers are made from proteins obtained from plants and function by forming a network that replaces the fat in food products. Lipid-based fat replacers are plant-based oils or fats that are modified to mimic the properties of traditional fats, providing similar taste and texture. These fat replacers are used to reduce unhealthy fats in food products while maintaining the desired characteristics.</p></p>
