<p><p>Adiabatic humidification systems are utilized to introduce moisture into an environment without external heat sources. The market offers four main types of systems. The ultrasonic type employs high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist. High pressure atomizing type utilizes a high-pressure pump to atomize water into tiny droplets. Air/Water atomizing type mixes water with compressed air to produce a controlled mist. Wetted media evaporative type draws air through a wetted medium, causing water to evaporate and humidify the surroundings. These systems facilitate humidity control in various industries and applications.</p></p>
