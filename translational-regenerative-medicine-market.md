<p><p>Translational regenerative medicine encompasses three main market types: stem cell therapy, tissue engineering, and gene therapy. Stem cell therapy involves using stem cells to replace or regenerate damaged cells or tissues. Tissue engineering utilizes a combination of cells, scaffolds, and growth factors to generate functional tissues or organs. Gene therapy aims to correct genetic abnormalities by introducing or modifying genes in the patient's cells. These market types all focus on utilizing advanced medical techniques to promote healing and restore normal function in patients.</p></p>
