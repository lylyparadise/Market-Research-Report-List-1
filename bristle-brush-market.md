<p><p>Bristle brushes are available in two main market types: natural bristle and artificial bristle. Natural bristle brushes are made from animal hair, such as boar or hog hair. They are known for their ability to hold and distribute paint or other substances evenly. Artificial bristle brushes, on the other hand, are made from synthetic materials like nylon or polyester. These brushes offer durability, flexibility, and are generally easier to clean. The choice between natural and artificial bristle brushes depends on individual preferences, painting techniques, and the specific application requirements.</p></p>
