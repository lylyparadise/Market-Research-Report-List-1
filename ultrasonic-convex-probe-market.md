<p><p>Ultrasonic convex probes are used in ultrasound imaging to produce high-resolution images of organs and tissues. The market for these probes includes three types: ultrasound straight probes, ultrasound angle probes, and ultrasound curvature probes. Ultrasound straight probes have a linear design and are suitable for scanning flat surfaces. Ultrasound angle probes have a curved shape, enabling scanning of curved surfaces. Ultrasound curvature probes have a convex design, allowing for a wider field of view and deeper tissue penetration. These different types of probes cater to various imaging needs and offer versatility in ultrasound diagnostics.</p></p>
