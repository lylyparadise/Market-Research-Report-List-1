<p><p>The automotive rain sensors market can be classified into two types: OEMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers) and the aftermarket. OEMs are integrated rain sensors that are pre-installed by vehicle manufacturers in their vehicles. These sensors are specifically designed and calibrated to meet the vehicle's specifications. On the other hand, aftermarket rain sensors are separate devices that can be installed in vehicles that do not have built-in rain sensors. These sensors are typically universal and can be installed by the vehicle owner or a third-party service provider.</p></p>
