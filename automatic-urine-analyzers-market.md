<p><p>Automatic Urine Analyzers are medical devices used to analyze urine samples for various parameters such as glucose, protein, pH levels, and specific gravity. These analyzers are available in two types: dry type and wet type. Dry type urine analyzers use test strips that are dipped into the urine sample and then inserted into the analyzer for automated analysis. Wet type urine analyzers, on the other hand, use reagent kits and measurement cells to directly analyze the urine sample. Both types provide efficient and accurate urine analysis results for medical diagnoses.</p></p>
