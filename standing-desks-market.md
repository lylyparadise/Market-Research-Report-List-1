<p><p>Standing desks are available in two main market types: fixed type and adjustable type. Fixed type standing desks are designed to remain at a fixed height, offering a stable surface for users who prefer to work while standing. These desks are suitable for individuals who have determined their preferred standing height or have no need to adjust the desk's height frequently. On the other hand, adjustable type standing desks feature a mechanism that allows the height to be easily modified. These desks cater to users who want the flexibility to switch between sitting and standing positions throughout the day.</p></p>
