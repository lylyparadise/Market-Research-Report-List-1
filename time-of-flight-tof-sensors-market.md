<p><p>Time-of-Flight sensors use different types of technology for distance measurement. RF-Modulated Light Sources use radio frequency signals to measure the time it takes for light to reflect back from an object. Range Gated Imagers emit short pulses of light and measure the time it takes for the light to return. Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers directly measure the time it takes for light to reflect back from an object. These different technologies cater to a variety of applications in industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
