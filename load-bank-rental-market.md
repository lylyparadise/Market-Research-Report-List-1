<p><strong>The Load Bank Rental Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Load Bank</li><li>Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Resistive/Reactive Load Bank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The load bank rental market consists of three main types: resistive load banks, reactive load banks, and resistive/reactive load banks. Resistive load banks are used to test power systems by providing a purely resistive load. Reactive load banks, on the other hand, imitate the inductive or capacitive loads found in real-world scenarios. Lastly, resistive/reactive load banks combine both resistive and reactive loads for more comprehensive testing. These load banks are commonly rented by businesses and organizations to ensure the proper functioning and performance of their power systems.</p></p>
