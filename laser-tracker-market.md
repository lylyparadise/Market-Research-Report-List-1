<p><p>A laser tracker is a portable precision measurement instrument used in industrial applications to accurately measure large objects and machinery. It utilizes a laser beam to track a target, reflecting the beam back to the tracker and calculating the distance based on the time it takes for the beam to return. Laser trackers are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, shipbuilding, and construction for applications such as alignment, calibration, inspection, and assembly.</p><p>The current outlook of the laser tracker market is positive and is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
