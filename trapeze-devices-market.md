<p><p>The trapeze devices market consists of two main types: ceiling-mounted and base stands. Ceiling-mounted trapeze devices are fixed to the ceiling, allowing for overhead support and movement. These devices are commonly used in hospitals and care facilities to assist patients in transferring from bed to wheelchair or from wheelchair to toilet. On the other hand, base stands are freestanding trapeze devices that can be placed beside the bed or chair. They provide stability and support for users during transfers and can be easily moved to different locations as needed.</p></p>
