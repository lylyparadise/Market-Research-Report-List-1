<p><p>Pitot Static Testers are essential tools used in aviation to measure and calibrate an aircraft's pitot-static system, which includes the pitot tubes and static ports. The pitot tube measures the aircraft's airspeed, while the static ports measure the atmospheric pressure. Pitot Static Testers ensure that these systems are functioning accurately to provide reliable airspeed and altitude information to pilots.</p><p>The global Pitot Static Testers market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue to expand at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period.
