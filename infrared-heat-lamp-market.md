<p><p>The Infrared Heat Lamp market can be divided into three types based on the wavelength of the infrared light they emit: Near Infrared, Medium Infrared, and Far Infrared. Near Infrared lamps produce shorter wavelength light which can penetrate the top layers of the skin, providing benefits such as pain relief and increased blood circulation. Medium Infrared lamps emit slightly longer wavelengths, capable of penetrating deeper tissues and muscles, offering therapeutic effects like muscle relaxation. Far Infrared lamps emit the longest wavelengths, deeply penetrating the body, resulting in benefits like detoxification and improved immune system.</p></p>
