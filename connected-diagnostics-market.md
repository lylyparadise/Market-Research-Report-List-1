<p><p>Connected Diagnostics is a market that comprises two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions involve installing and maintaining diagnostic systems within the user's own infrastructure, allowing them to have complete control and security over their data. Cloud-Based solutions, on the other hand, rely on remote servers to store, process, and analyze diagnostic information. These solutions offer greater scalability, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness, as they eliminate the need for extensive hardware installations and provide real-time access to diagnostics from anywhere.</p></p>
