<p><p>Automated pipetting systems are divided into two main types: standalone benchtop workstations and multi-instrument systems. Standalone benchtop workstations are compact devices designed for individual use on a laboratory bench, offering precision and efficiency for pipetting tasks. On the other hand, multi-instrument systems are larger setups that can handle multiple instruments simultaneously, increasing throughput and productivity in high-volume laboratories. Both types offer automated solutions for pipetting tasks, but cater to different scales of operation and laboratory requirements.</p></p>
