<p><p>The lawn mower batteries market includes two main types: Lead-Acid Batteries and Li-Ion Batteries. Lead-Acid Batteries are commonly used due to their lower cost and high power delivery capacity. They are relatively heavy and require regular maintenance, including checking the battery's electrolyte level and keeping it charged. On the other hand, Li-Ion Batteries are lighter and more compact, making them a popular choice for cordless lawn mowers. They offer longer run times, faster charging, and don't require regular maintenance. However, they are relatively more expensive than Lead-Acid Batteries.</p></p>
