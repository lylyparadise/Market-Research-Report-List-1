<p><p>A passive desiccant wheel refers to a device used in air conditioning systems to remove moisture from the air. This process helps in increasing the cooling capacity of the air conditioning system.</p><p>The future of the passive desiccant wheel market looks promising, with an expected CAGR of 7.9% during the forecasted period.
