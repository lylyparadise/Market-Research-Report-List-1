<p><strong>The ICP Etchers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Load</li><li>Lock-Load</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ICP Etchers market can be classified into two main types based on the loading mechanism: Open-Load and Lock-Load. In Open-Load systems, the wafer is loaded into the chamber without being locked in place, allowing for easy access during processing. On the other hand, Lock-Load systems require the wafer to be securely locked in place before processing begins to ensure precise etching. Each type offers unique advantages and disadvantages, catering to different requirements and preferences in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
