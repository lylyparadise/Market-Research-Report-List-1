<p><p>Sewage treatment facilities aim to treat and purify wastewater before it is released back into the environment. Moving Bed Biofilm Reactors (MBBR) involve the use of suspended plastic media that provide a surface for the growth of microorganisms, enhancing the treatment process. Ozone-Based Advanced Oxidation utilizes ozone to break down and remove pollutants from wastewater. Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) is commonly used for the removal of organic substances from wastewater, as the powdered carbon absorbs and removes impurities. All these technologies contribute towards efficient sewage treatment.</p></p>
