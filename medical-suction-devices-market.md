<p><ul><li>Consumable</li><li>Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical suction devices market can be divided into two types: consumable and equipment markets. The consumable market refers to the segment where disposable items such as catheters, tubing, and canisters are used for suction procedures. These items are used once and then discarded. On the other hand, the equipment market involves the sale of reusable and durable suction devices, such as vacuum pumps and aspirators, which can be used multiple times. Both types cater to the needs of healthcare facilities and professionals for effective suctioning during medical procedures.</p></p>
