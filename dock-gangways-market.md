<p><p>Dock gangways are essential for safe access between a dock and a vessel. Market types for dock gangways can be categorized into two: with handrails and without handrails. Gangways with handrails provide an extra layer of safety, ensuring stability and preventing accidental falls. They are ideal for locations that require heightened safety measures, such as shipyards or areas with high foot traffic. On the other hand, gangways without handrails are simpler in design and more suitable for locations with lower safety requirements or restricted space. Both types serve their purpose based on the specific safety needs of different environments.</p></p>
