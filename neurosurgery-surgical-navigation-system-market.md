<p><p>The Neurosurgery Surgical Navigation System market comprises two types, namely Optical and Electromagnetic. Optical navigation systems use cameras and infrared light to track markers placed on the patient's head and instruments, providing real-time guidance during surgery. On the other hand, electromagnetic navigation systems employ electromagnetic fields to track the position of surgical instruments. Both systems enable surgeons to accurately navigate through the delicate brain structures, aiding in precise incisions and reducing risks during neurosurgical procedures.</p></p>
