<p><strong>The Injection Test Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Injection Test Equipment</li><li>Secondary Injection Test Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Injection test equipment is used in electrical systems to test the performance and reliability of protective devices such as circuit breakers. Primary injection test equipment applies high currents directly to the electrical power system, simulating real-world conditions. On the other hand, secondary injection test equipment verifies the functionality of protective devices by injecting low-level currents into their secondary side. Both types of equipment are crucial in ensuring the proper functioning and safety of electrical systems by testing the response of protective devices under different operating conditions.</p></p>
