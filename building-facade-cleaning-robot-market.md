<p><p>Building Facade Cleaning Robot market is divided into Contact Cleaning Robot and Non-contact Cleaning Robot types. Contact Cleaning Robots are equipped with brushes or abrasive pads, which physically scrub the surface of the building facade to remove dirt and grime. On the other hand, Non-contact Cleaning Robots use high-pressure water jets or chemical solutions to clean the facade without direct contact. Contact Cleaning Robots are suitable for moderate to heavy stain removal, while Non-contact Cleaning Robots are preferred for delicate surfaces or areas with limited accessibility.</p></p>
