<p><p>The UHP heat exchangers market includes two types: metal shell heat exchangers and non-metallic shell heat exchangers. Metal shell heat exchangers are made of metals such as stainless steel or titanium, which offer high durability and resistance to corrosion. They are commonly used in industries with high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Non-metallic shell heat exchangers, on the other hand, are constructed using materials like fiberglass or plastic. They are preferred in industries where corrosion is a concern or when weight reduction is desired while maintaining high thermal efficiency.</p></p>
