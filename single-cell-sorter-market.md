<p><p>The single cell sorter market consists of two main types: benchtop cell sorters and compact cell sorters. Benchtop cell sorters are designed for smaller laboratories or research facilities, offering high-precision sorting capabilities for a wide range of cell types. They require less space and have user-friendly interfaces, making them suitable for routine cell analysis. On the other hand, compact cell sorters are portable and easy to transport, making them suitable for on-site or field research purposes. They are often used in remote locations or resource-limited settings where access to larger laboratory equipment is limited.</p></p>
