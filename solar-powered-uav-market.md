<p><p>The solar-powered UAV market consists of three main types of unmanned aerial vehicles: Fixed-wing UAVs, rotorcraft UAVs, and umbrella UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs are designed with a fixed wing structure, resembling conventional airplanes, allowing for longer flight durations and larger payloads. Rotorcraft UAVs, on the other hand, use rotating blades to achieve vertical take-off and landing, offering more flexibility in confined spaces. Umbrella UAVs have a unique umbrella-like shape, enabling them to harvest solar energy efficiently and fly for extended periods, particularly useful for surveillance and communication applications.</p></p>
