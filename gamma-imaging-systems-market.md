<p><ul><li>Measurement Type</li><li>Detection Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gamma imaging systems are medical devices used to capture images of the internal organs and tissues. The market for these systems can be categorized based on two types: measurement type and detection type. Measurement type gamma imaging systems include single-photon emission computed tomography (SPECT) and positron emission tomography (PET). These systems measure the amount and distribution of radioactive tracers in the body. On the other hand, detection type gamma imaging systems use gamma cameras to detect and capture gamma radiation emitted by the radioactive tracers, providing detailed images for diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
