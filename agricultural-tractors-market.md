<p><p>The agricultural tractors market is categorized into two types: Two-Wheel Drive and Four-Wheel Drive. Two-Wheel Drive tractors are equipped with power only to two wheels, usually the rear ones. They are suitable for flat and dry terrains. In contrast, Four-Wheel Drive tractors have power distributed to all four wheels, providing enhanced traction and maneuverability.
