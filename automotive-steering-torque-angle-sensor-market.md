<p><p>Automotive Steering Torque & Angle Sensors come in three main types - Steering Mount Sensor, Steering Base Mount Sensor, and Steering Column Mount Sensor. Steering Mount Sensors are installed directly on the steering wheel, Steering Base Mount Sensors are mounted on the steering column base, and Steering Column Mount Sensors are placed on the steering column itself. These sensors measure the torque and angle of the steering wheel to provide accurate feedback to the vehicle's steering system, ensuring precise control and safety while driving.</p></p>
