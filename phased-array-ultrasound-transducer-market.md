<p><ul><li>Linear Array</li><li>Area Array</li><li>Ring Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phased array ultrasound transducer market consists of various types, including linear array, area array, and ring array. Linear array transducers use multiple adjacent transducer elements to create a rectangular image with good resolution. Area array transducers utilize multiple elements arranged in a curvilinear shape for a wider field of view. Ring array transducers consist of concentric rings of elements, allowing for sector imaging and 3D imaging capabilities. These different types cater to specific imaging needs, offering versatility and customization in the ultrasound imaging field.</p></p>
