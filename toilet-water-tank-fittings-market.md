<p><p>Toilet water tank fittings are components used in the construction of toilets. They can be categorized into four main types: fill valve, flush valve, push button and lever, and others. </p><p>Fill valves regulate the water flow into the toilet tank, ensuring it reaches the desired level. Flush valves control the release of water from the tank to initiate the flushing process. Push buttons and levers are manual mechanisms used to activate the flush. Lastly, the "others" category includes additional fittings such as overflow tubes, rubber seals, and gaskets used to maintain the proper functioning of the toilet water tank.</p></p>
