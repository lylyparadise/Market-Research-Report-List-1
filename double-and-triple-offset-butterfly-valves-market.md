They provide a good seal and are suitable for applications that require tight shut-off.</p><p>On the other hand, triple offset butterfly valves have an eccentric disc that is designed to create a bubble-tight seal. They are preferred for high-pressure and high-temperature applications where a reliable shut-off is crucial.</p><p>Both types of valves offer advantages in terms of durability and performance, but triple offset butterfly valves are often used in more demanding environments due to their enhanced sealing capabilities.</p></p>
