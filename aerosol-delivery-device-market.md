<p><p>The aerosol delivery device market includes three main types: dry powder inhalers (DPIs), metered dose inhalers (MDIs), and nebulizers. DPIs use a powdered form of medication that the patient inhales, while MDIs deliver liquid medication in a metered dose through a pressurized container. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a fine mist that is inhaled by the patient. These devices are commonly used to treat respiratory conditions like asthma and COPD, providing an efficient and convenient way to deliver medication directly to the lungs.</p></p>
